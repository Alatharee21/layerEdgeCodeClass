To DoList

  • Plan the day - Write down priorities and set time blocks for each task.

  • Check emails/messages - Respond to important ones and archive or delete the rest.

  • Complete main work task - Focus on one high-impact task before midday.

  • Take a 10-minute break - Step away from your desk to rest your eyes and mind.

  • Review progress - At the end of the day, note what's done and carry over pending tasks.
