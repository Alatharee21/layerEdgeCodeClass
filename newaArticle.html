Small public companies snap up ether in new crypto gold rush, even as risks linger

We are so back as Vitalink becomes the richest man in Crypto space

August 5, 2025

A growing number of small, publicly traded companies are turning to Ether as a strategic inflation hedge—holding approximately 966,304 ETH (around $3.5 billion) in their treasuries, compared to under 116,000 ETH at the end of 2024. They're drawn to ETH for its growth potential and staking yields (3–4%), especially in decentralized finance (DeFi). Yet analysts caution that this surge may echo past speculative frenzies, with regulatory concerns and volatility still looming.

Bitcoin Advances As Trump Adds Crypto To 401(k) Plans

Make Crypto great again

August 7, 2025

U.S. President Donald Trump signed an executive order permitting 401(k) plans to include alternative assets such as cryptocurrencies. This landmark policy shift gave a strong boost to digital asset markets: Bitcoin rose nearly 2% to above $117,300, while Ethereum jumped over 5%, exceeding $3,800. The move opens up the vast $43 trillion retirement market to crypto investors and follows broader regulatory advances like the GENIUS Act and ETF developments.
